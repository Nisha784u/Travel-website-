Welcome to Dehradun

Robber's Cave

Robber's Cave is a natural cave formation near Dehradun. It is also known as Gucchupani. The cave is famous for its stream that mysteriously disappears underground and reappears a few meters away.

Sahastradhara

Sahastradhara is a beautiful waterfall situated in the caves. The name Sahastradhara means 'The Thousand-fold Spring.' It is known for its therapeutic value as the water is believed to have medicinal properties.

Mindrolling Monastery

Mindrolling Monastery is one of the largest Buddhist centers in India. The monastery complex is adorned with beautiful gardens, stupas, and a 60-meter high Buddha statue.

Forest Research Institute

The Forest Research Institute (FRI) is a premier institution for forestry research in India. It has a vast campus with impressive colonial architecture and a museum showcasing forestry-related exhibits.
